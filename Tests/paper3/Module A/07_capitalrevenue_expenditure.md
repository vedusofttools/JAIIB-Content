## 1.
Expenditure incurred to acquire a fixed asset is classified as:

A. Revenue Expenditure  
B. Capital Expenditure  
C. Deferred Revenue Expenditure  
D. Contingent Expenditure

Answer: B

Explanation:
Capital expenditure is incurred to acquire or improve fixed assets and provides benefit beyond one accounting period.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.

A. Revenue Expenditure  
B. Capital Expenditure  
C. Deferred Expenditure  
D. Contingent Liability

Answer: B

Explanation:
Expenses directly attributable to bringing asset to working condition are capitalised.

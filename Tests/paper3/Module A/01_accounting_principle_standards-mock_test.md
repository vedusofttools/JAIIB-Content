## 1.
A bank purchased office furniture for ₹5,00,000. Installation cost was ₹50,000 and transportation ₹20,000. At what amount should the asset be capitalised as per AS 10?

A. ₹5,00,000  
B. ₹5,50,000  
C. ₹5,70,000  
D. ₹6,00,000

Answer: C

Explanation:  
As per AS 10 (Property, Plant & Equipment), all directly attributable costs necessary to bring the asset to working condition must be capitalised.  
Total = 5,00,000 + 50,000 + 20,000 = ₹5,70,000.

## 2.
A company changed its depreciation method from WDV to SLM as it felt SLM better reflects usage. This change is treated as:

A. Change in estimate  
B. Change in accounting policy  
C. Prior period item  
D. Extraordinary item

Answer: A

Explanation:  
As per AS 10 and AS 5, change in depreciation method is treated as a change in accounting estimate, applied prospectively.

## 5.
A machine costing ₹10,00,000 has residual value ₹1,00,000 and useful life 9 years. Annual depreciation under SLM:

A. ₹1,11,111  
B. ₹1,00,000  
C. ₹90,000  
D. ₹1,20,000

Answer: B

Explanation:  
Depreciable amount = 10,00,000 – 1,00,000 = 9,00,000  
Annual Depreciation = 9,00,000 / 9 = ₹1,00,000.

# Bank Audit and Inspection
Total Questions: 30  
Each Question Carries 1 Mark  
Choose the most appropriate answer.  
Detailed explanation provided after each question.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.
The statutory audit of a bank in India is conducted under the provisions of:

A. RBI Act  
B. Banking Regulation Act  
C. Companies Act  
D. Negotiable Instruments Act

Answer: B

Explanation:
Statutory audit of banking companies is governed by the Banking Regulation Act. RBI appoints auditors in consultation with the Central Government.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
The primary objective of bank audit is to:

A. Detect fraud only  
B. Verify compliance and true & fair view of financial statements  
C. Increase profits  
D. Approve loans

Explanation:
The objective is to ensure financial statements present a true and fair view and comply with regulatory guidelines.

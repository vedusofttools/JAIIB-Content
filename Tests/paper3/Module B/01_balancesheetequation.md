## 1.
Assets ₹12,00,000; Liabilities ₹7,50,000. Profit ₹1,00,000; Drawings ₹50,000. Opening capital?

A. ₹3,00,000  
B. ₹4,00,000  
C. ₹3,50,000  
D. ₹5,00,000

Answer: B

Explanation:
Closing Capital = 12,00,000 – 7,50,000 = 4,50,000  
Opening = Closing – Profit + Drawings  
= 4,50,000 – 1,00,000 + 50,000 = ₹4,00,000

## 3.
Opening Capital ₹6,00,000; Closing Capital ₹7,20,000; Drawings ₹60,000. Profit?

A. ₹1,20,000  
B. ₹1,80,000  
C. ₹60,000  
D. ₹2,40,000

Answer: B

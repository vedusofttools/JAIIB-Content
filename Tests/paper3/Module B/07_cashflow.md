## 1.
Cash Flow Statement is prepared as per:

A. AS 1  
B. AS 2  
C. AS 3  
D. AS 10

Answer: C

Explanation:
AS 3 prescribes the format and classification of cash flows into operating, investing, and financing activities.

------------------------------------------------------------

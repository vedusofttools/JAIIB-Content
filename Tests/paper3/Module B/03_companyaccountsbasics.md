# Company Accounts – Basics
Total Questions: 30  
Each Question Carries 1 Mark  
Choose the most appropriate answer.  
Detailed explanation provided after each question.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.
The capital of a company is divided into:

A. Units  
B. Shares  
C. Debentures  
D. Bonds

Answer: B

Explanation:
The capital of a company is divided into small equal units called shares.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
Authorised capital is:

A. Capital issued to public  
B. Maximum capital company is authorised to issue  
C. Paid-up capital  
D. Reserve capital

Answer: B

Explanation:
Authorised capital is the maximum amount of share capital that a company is permitted to issue as per its Memorandum.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.
Issued capital is:

A. Capital offered to public for subscription  
B. Maximum capital  
C. Paid capital  
D. Called-up capital

Answer: A

Explanation:
Issued capital is that portion of authorised capital offered to investors.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 4.
Subscribed capital refers to:

A. Total authorised capital  
B. Shares applied for by public  
C. Paid-up amount only  
D. Uncalled amount

Answer: B

Explanation:
Subscribed capital is the portion of issued capital subscribed by investors.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 5.
Paid-up capital is:

A. Amount called by company  
B. Amount actually paid by shareholders  
C. Maximum capital  
D. Reserve capital

Answer: B

Explanation:
Paid-up capital represents actual amount received from shareholders.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 6.
Share capital shown in Balance Sheet includes:

A. Authorised capital  
B. Issued capital  
C. Paid-up capital  
D. Subscribed capital only

Answer: C

Explanation:
Balance Sheet shows paid-up capital under Shareholders’ Funds.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 7.
Preference shares have:

A. Voting rights equal to equity

C. No dividend

Preference shareholders get priority in dividend and repayment of capital.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 8.
Equity shareholders are:

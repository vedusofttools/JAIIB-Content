# Preparation of Final Accounts
Total Questions: 30  
Each Question Carries 1 Mark  
Choose the most appropriate answer.  
Detailed explanation provided after each question.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.
Final Accounts of a sole proprietorship consist of:

A. Journal and Ledger  
B. Trading Account, Profit & Loss Account, and Balance Sheet  
C. Cash Book and Trial Balance  
D. Bank Reconciliation Statement

Answer: B

Explanation:
Final accounts include Trading Account (to determine gross profit), Profit & Loss Account (to determine net profit), and Balance Sheet (to show financial position).

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
Gross Profit is calculated as:

A. Sales – Expenses  
B. Sales – Cost of Goods Sold  
C. Assets – Liabilities  
D. Capital – Drawings

Answer: B

Explanation:
Gross Profit = Net Sales – Cost of Goods Sold (COGS).

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.
Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) is calculated as:

A. Opening Stock + Purchases – Closing Stock  
B. Purchases – Closing Stock  
C. Sales – Purchases  
D. Opening Stock – Purchases

Answer: A

Explanation:
COGS = Opening Stock + Net Purchases – Closing Stock.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 8.

## 1.
An exchange rate quoted as USD/INR 83.50 means:

A. ₹83.50 per USD  
B. $83.50 per ₹1  
C. ₹1 per $83.50  
D. ₹83.50 per €1

Answer: A

Explanation:
USD/INR 83.50 means 1 US Dollar = ₹83.50.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
If USD/INR rises from 83 to 84, it indicates:

A. Rupee appreciation  
B. Rupee depreciation  
C. Dollar depreciation  
D. No change

Answer: B

Explanation:
More rupees required per USD → rupee has depreciated.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.
Direct quotation in India is:

A. INR per unit of foreign currency  
B. Foreign currency per INR  
C. Euro per USD  
D. GBP per USD

Answer: A

Explanation:
India follows direct quotation: ₹ per foreign currency.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 4.
Indirect quotation is:

A. ₹ per foreign currency

## 18.
Importer needs $10,000. Quote 84.00/84.20. Rupees payable?

A. ₹8,40,000  
B. ₹8,42,000  
C. ₹8,38,000  
D. ₹8,50,000

## 22.

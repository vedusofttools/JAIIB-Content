## 1.
Costing method refers to:

A. Pricing policy  
B. Technique used to ascertain cost of a product or service  
C. Audit system  
D. Tax calculation

Answer: B

Explanation:
Costing methods are systems used to determine the cost of products or services depending on the nature of production.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
Job costing is suitable for:

A. Continuous production  
B. Mass production  
C. Customized or specific jobs  
D. Oil refineries

Answer: C

Explanation:
Job costing is used where production is based on specific customer orders.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.
Batch costing is a variation of:

A. Process costing  
B. Job costing  
C. Contract costing  
D. Service costing

Answer: B

Explanation:
Batch costing is similar to job costing but applies to a batch of identical units.

Process costing is used where production is continuous and homogeneous (e.g., cement, chemicals).

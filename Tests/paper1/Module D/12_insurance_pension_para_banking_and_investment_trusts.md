# Insurance Products, Pension Funds, Para Banking and Investment Trusts

# Insurance Products

## Meaning of Insurance

Insurance is a contract where:

- Risk is transferred from insured to insurer
- Premium is paid
- Compensation is provided in case of loss

---

## Fundamental Principles of Insurance

- Utmost good faith
- Insurable interest
- Indemnity
- Subrogation
- Contribution
- Proximate cause

---

## Classification of Insurance

### Life Insurance
- Covers risk of death
- Long-term savings component

---

### General Insurance
- Health
- Motor
- Fire
- Marine

---

## Group Insurance Schemes

- Covers group of people
- Common in employer-employee setup
- Lower premium due to risk pooling

---

## Micro Insurance

- Small premium policies
- Targeted at low-income groups
- Supports financial inclusion

---

## Insurance Based Social Security Schemes

### PMJJBY (Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana)

- Life insurance scheme
- Low annual premium
- Coverage in case of death

---

### PMSBY (Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana)

- Accidental insurance scheme
- Low premium
- Coverage for accidental death or disability

---

## Bancassurance

- Banks distribute insurance products
- Improves penetration
- Additional fee-based income for banks

---

## Insurance Ombudsman Scheme

- Redressal mechanism for policyholders
- Handles disputes and complaints

---

# Pension Funds

## Meaning of Pension System

Pension system provides:

- Regular income after retirement
- Financial security in old age

---

## Types of Pension Schemes

- Defined benefit schemes
- Defined contribution schemes

---

## Employeesâ€™ Provident Fund (EPF)

- Mandatory for eligible employees
- Contributions by employer and employee
- Long-term retirement savings

---

## Public Provident Fund (PPF)

- Government-backed savings scheme
- Long-term tenure
- Tax benefits

---

## National Pension System (NPS)

- Introduced in 2004 (for government employees)
- Open to all citizens
- Regulated by PFRDA
- Market-linked returns

---

## Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

- Targeted at unorganised sector
- Guaranteed pension after 60 years
- Government-supported scheme

---

# Para Banking and Financial Services by Banks

## Meaning

Para banking refers to:

- Financial services provided by banks
- Other than traditional deposit and lending

---

## Types of Para Banking Services

- Mutual fund distribution
- Insurance distribution
- Portfolio management
- Merchant banking
- Credit cards
- Wealth management

---

## Organisation of Para Banking

Banks may:

- Provide services directly
- Through subsidiaries
- Through joint ventures

---

## Disclosure of Commission

Banks must:

- Disclose commissions earned
- Maintain transparency
- Avoid conflict of interest

---

# Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)

## Meaning

REITs are investment vehicles that:

- Invest in income-generating real estate
- Allow investors to earn rental income
- Trade on stock exchanges

---

## Organisation of REITs

- Sponsor
- Trustee
- Asset manager

---

## Types of REITs

- Equity REIT
- Mortgage REIT
- Hybrid REIT

---

## Advantages

- Liquidity
- Diversification
- Regular income

---

## Regulatory Framework

- Regulated by SEBI
- Must meet asset and disclosure norms

---

# Infrastructure Investment Trusts (InvITs)

## Meaning

InvITs are:

- Investment vehicles for infrastructure projects
- Pool funds from investors
- Invest in infrastructure assets

---

## Purpose

- Fund infrastructure development
- Provide steady income to investors

---

## Types

- Public InvIT
- Private InvIT

---

## Revenue Model

- Toll collections
- User charges
- Long-term contracts

---

## Taxation and Regulation

- Regulated by SEBI
- Subject to tax guidelines for investors

---

# Importance for Banking

- Fee-based income generation
- Investment advisory services
- Cross-selling opportunities
- Long-term savings mobilisation

---

# Relevance for JAIIB

- PMJJBY vs PMSBY frequently asked
- NPS vs APY difference common
- REIT vs InvIT difference tested
- Para banking meaning important
- Fundamental principles of insurance often examined

---

# Key Facts

- NPS introduced: 2004
- PFRDA Act: 2013
- PMJJBY and PMSBY launched in 2015
- REITs and InvITs regulated by SEBI

---

# Common Exam Traps

- Confusing NPS with EPF
- Treating REIT as mutual fund
- Assuming para banking includes deposit taking
- Ignoring difference between PMSBY and PMJJBY

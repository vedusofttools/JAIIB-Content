## 1.
Merchant banking primarily deals with:

A. Retail deposit mobilization  
B. Corporate financial advisory and issue management  
C. Agricultural credit  
D. Microfinance

**Answer: B**

**Explanation:**  
Merchant bankers provide services like issue management, underwriting, corporate advisory, mergers & acquisitions, and portfolio management. They do not accept public deposits like banks.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
Merchant bankers in India are regulated by:

A. RBI  
B. SEBI  
C. IRDAI  
D. PFRDA

**Answer: B**

**Explanation:**  
Merchant bankers are regulated under SEBI (Merchant Bankers) Regulations, 1992.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.

A. Provide loan to investors

**Explanation:**

## 4.

**Explanation:**

## 5.

A. ₹25 crore

**Explanation:**

## 1.
Microfinance primarily provides financial services to:

A. Large corporates  
B. High net worth individuals  
C. Low-income households  
D. Foreign investors

Answer: C

Explanation:
Microfinance aims to provide credit, savings, insurance, and other services to economically weaker sections excluded from formal banking.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
The Self-Help Group (SHG) – Bank Linkage Programme was launched by:

A. RBI  
B. NABARD  
C. SEBI  
D. SIDBI

Answer: B

Explanation:
NABARD initiated SHG–Bank linkage in 1992 to promote financial inclusion.

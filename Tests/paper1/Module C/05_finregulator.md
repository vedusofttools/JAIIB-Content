## 1.
The apex monetary authority of India is:

A. SEBI  
B. IRDAI  
C. RBI  
D. PFRDA

Answer: C

Explanation:
Reserve Bank of India (RBI) regulates monetary policy, banks, NBFCs, and payment systems under RBI Act, 1934.

------------------------------------------------------------

A. Banking system

Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) regulates stock exchanges, brokers, mutual funds, and capital markets.

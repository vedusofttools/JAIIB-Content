### PART A – THEORY (1–10)
############################

## 1.
Law of Demand states that:

A. Price and quantity demanded move in same direction  
B. Price and quantity demanded move in opposite direction  
C. Demand is constant  
D. Income determines price

Answer: B

Explanation:
As price increases, quantity demanded decreases, ceteris paribus.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
A rightward shift of demand curve indicates:

A. Decrease in demand  
B. Increase in demand  
C. Increase in price  
D. Contraction of demand

Answer: B

Explanation:
Shift right means more quantity demanded at every price.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.
Movement along demand curve occurs due to change in:

A. Income  
B. Taste  
C. Price of the good  
D. Population

Answer: C

Explanation:
Change in price causes expansion or contraction along the curve.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 4.
Law of Supply states that:

A. Price and supply move inversely  
B. Price and supply move directly  
C. Supply is constant  
D. Income determines supply

Answer: B

Explanation:
Higher price encourages producers to supply more.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 5.
Equilibrium price is determined where:

A. Demand > Supply  
B. Supply > Demand  
C. Demand = Supply  
D. Income = Price

Answer: C

Explanation:
Market equilibrium occurs where quantity demanded equals quantity supplied.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 6.
Price elasticity of demand measures:

A. Change in supply  
B. Responsiveness of demand to price change  
C. Income change  
D. Cost change

Answer: B

Explanation:
Elasticity measures % change in quantity demanded due to % change in price.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 7.
If elasticity > 1, demand is:

A. Inelastic  
B. Unit elastic  
C. Elastic  
D. Perfectly inelastic

Answer: C

Explanation:
Elastic demand means quantity changes more than price.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 8.
If elasticity < 1, demand is:

A. Elastic  
B. Inelastic  
C. Unit elastic  
D. Perfectly elastic

Answer: B

Explanation:
Inelastic demand shows less responsiveness to price change.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 9.
Perfectly inelastic demand curve is:

A. Horizontal  
B. Vertical  
C. Downward sloping  
D. Upward sloping

Answer: B

Explanation:
Quantity remains constant regardless of price.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 10.
Perfectly elastic demand curve is:

A. Vertical  
B. Horizontal  
C. Downward sloping  
D. Upward sloping

Answer: B

Explanation:
Small change in price leads to infinite change in quantity.

## 21.
Initial price ₹50, new price ₹60. Quantity falls from 100 to 80. Elasticity?

A. 0.5  
B. 1  
C. 2  
D. 3

Answer: B

Explanation:
% change in Q = 20/100 = 20%  
% change in P = 10/50 = 20%  
Elasticity = 1 (unit elastic).

------------------------------------------------------------

## 22.
If elasticity = 2 and price increases 5%, quantity falls by:

A. 2%  
B. 5%  
C. 10%  
D. 15%

Answer: C

Explanation:
Elasticity = %ΔQ / %ΔP  
2 = Q / 5%  
Q = 10%.

------------------------------------------------------------

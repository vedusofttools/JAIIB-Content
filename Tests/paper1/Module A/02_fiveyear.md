## 1.
India’s First Five Year Plan was launched in:

A. 1947  
B. 1950  
C. 1951  
D. 1955

Answer: C

Explanation:
The First Five Year Plan was launched in 1951 and covered the period 1951–1956.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.
MSME classification (as per current norms) is based on:

A. Turnover only  
B. Investment only  
C. Investment and Turnover  
D. Number of employees only

Answer: C

Explanation:
MSME classification in India is based on both investment in plant & machinery/equipment and annual turnover.

------------------------------------------------------------

Micro enterprises have investment up to ₹1 crore and turnover up to ₹5 crore.

### PART A – Theory (1–20)
############################

## 1.
The Indian economy is classified as:

A. Capitalist economy  
B. Socialist economy  
C. Mixed economy  
D. Closed economy

Answer: C

Explanation:
India follows a mixed economy model where both public and private sectors coexist.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
The three major sectors of the Indian economy are:

A. Agriculture, Banking, Trade  
B. Primary, Secondary, Tertiary  
C. Rural, Urban, Foreign  
D. Public, Private, Joint

Answer: B

Explanation:
The economy is divided into Primary (agriculture), Secondary (industry), and Tertiary (services) sectors.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.
The Primary sector includes:

A. Manufacturing  
B. Mining and Agriculture  
C. Banking  
D. IT services

Answer: B

Explanation:
Primary sector deals with extraction of natural resources like agriculture and mining.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 4.
The Secondary sector mainly includes:

A. Services  
B. Agriculture  
C. Manufacturing and Industry  
D. Trade

Answer: C

Explanation:
Secondary sector involves processing and manufacturing activities.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 5.
The Tertiary sector includes:

A. Mining  
B. Manufacturing  
C. Services like banking and IT  
D. Agriculture

Answer: C

Explanation:
Tertiary sector provides services such as banking, insurance, IT, transport.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 6.
The largest contributor to India’s GDP currently is:

A. Agriculture  
B. Industry  
C. Services

Answer: C

Explanation:
The service sector contributes the highest share to India’s GDP.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 7.

B. 25–30%

Agriculture contributes around 14–16% of GDP though it employs a larger workforce.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 8.
Structural transformation refers to:

A. Growth in agriculture only  
B. Shift from agriculture to industry and services  
C. Increase in imports  
D. Reduction in GDP

Answer: B

Explanation:
As economy develops, contribution shifts from primary to secondary and tertiary sectors.

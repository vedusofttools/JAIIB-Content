## 1.
Retail banking contributes to bank operations mainly by:

A. Providing large corporate loans  
B. Generating stable interest and fee income  
C. Investing in government securities only  
D. Managing foreign exchange reserves

Answer: B

Explanation:
Retail banking generates stable income through diversified small loans and service charges.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 2.
One major operational advantage of retail banking is:

A. High concentration risk  
B. Risk diversification  
C. Dependence on one borrower  
D. Seasonal income only

Answer: B

Explanation:
Retail banking spreads risk across many borrowers, reducing concentration risk.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 3.
Retail deposits form a significant part of:

A. Bank’s capital  
B. Bank’s liabilities  
C. Bank’s fixed assets  
D. Bank’s reserves only

Answer: B

Explanation:
Deposits (especially CASA) are liabilities and key funding sources.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 4.
High CASA ratio improves:

A. Cost of funds

C. Tax liability  
D. Risk exposure

Answer: B

Explanation:
CASA deposits are low-cost, improving net interest margin (NIM).

------------------------------------------------------------

## 21.
If CASA increases from 30% to 45%, bank’s cost of funds will:

A. Increase  
B. Decrease  
C. Remain same  
D. Double

Answer: B

Explanation:
Higher CASA means more low-cost deposits → reduced cost of funds.

------------------------------------------------------------

## 22.

A. Severe  
B. Moderate  
C. Minimal  
D. Insolvent

Answer: C

Explanation:
Small ticket size reduces impact on total portfolio.

# Forex Market and Foreign Exchange Framework

## Meaning of Forex Market

The Foreign Exchange (Forex) Market is a market where:

- Foreign currencies are bought and sold
- Exchange rates are determined
- International payments are settled

It facilitates:
- International trade
- Foreign investment
- Capital flows
- Remittances

---

## Evolution of Forex Market in India

### Pre-1991 Phase
- Highly regulated
- Controlled exchange rate system
- Foreign Exchange Regulation Act (FERA), 1973

---

### Post-1991 Reforms
- Liberalisation of exchange rate regime
- Move towards market-determined exchange rate
- Partial convertibility (1992)
- Current account convertibility (1994)

---

### FEMA, 1999

- Foreign Exchange Management Act enacted in 1999
- Replaced FERA
- Objective shifted from control to management

FEMA came into effect on 1 June 2000.

---

## Characteristics of Forex Market

- Largest financial market globally
- 24-hour trading
- Highly liquid
- Decentralised (OTC market)
- Sensitive to global events

---

## Market Participants

- Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
- Authorised Dealer Banks
- Corporates
- Exporters and importers
- Foreign Institutional Investors
- Retail participants

---

## Exchange Rate System in India

India follows:

- Managed floating exchange rate system

RBI intervenes to:
- Reduce volatility
- Maintain orderly conditions
- Prevent excessive depreciation/appreciation

---

# LIBOR and Alternate Reference Rates (ARRs)

## LIBOR

- London Interbank Offered Rate
- Global benchmark interest rate
- Used in international borrowing

LIBOR phased out after 2021 due to manipulation concerns.

---

## Alternate Reference Rates (ARRs)

Examples:
- SOFR (USA)
- SONIA (UK)

Used as replacement for LIBOR.

---

# Foreign Exchange Dealers Association of India (FEDAI)

## Meaning

FEDAI is an association of banks dealing in foreign exchange.

---

## Role

- Sets operational guidelines
- Standardises forex practices
- Ensures uniformity in dealings

---

# FX-Retail Platform

- Allows retail participation in forex market
- Improves transparency
- Facilitates small investors

---

# US Dollar Index (USDX)

## Meaning

USDX measures:

- Value of US Dollar against basket of major currencies

---

## Importance

- Indicator of global dollar strength
- Influences capital flows and commodity prices

---

# ADR and GDR

## American Depository Receipt (ADR)

- Issued in USA
- Represents shares of foreign company
- Traded on US exchanges

---

## Global Depository Receipt (GDR)

- Issued in international markets
- Represents shares of foreign company
- Traded outside USA

---

## Purpose

- Raise foreign capital
- Access international investors
- Improve global visibility

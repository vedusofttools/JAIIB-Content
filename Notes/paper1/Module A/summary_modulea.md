# Summary Module A

## Structure of Indian Economy
- Nature: Mixed economy
- Sectors:
    - Primary → agriculture, mining
    - Secondary → manufacturing, construction
    - Tertiary → services
- GDP contribution:
    - Services → highest
    - Industry → moderate
    - Agriculture → lowest
- Employment:
    - Agriculture → highest employment
- Structural imbalance → high agri employment, low GDP share

---

## National Income & GDP
- GDP → value of goods & services within country
- GNP → GDP + net factor income from abroad
- NNP → GNP – depreciation
- Per capita income → national income ÷ population
- Real income → adjusted for inflation
- Nominal income → current prices

## Priority Sector Lending (PSL)

### Introduction
- Introduced: 1972
- Regulator: RBI
- Overall target: 40% of ANBC

### Sub-targets
- Agriculture: 18%
- Small & Marginal Farmers: 8%
- Micro enterprises: 7.5%
- Weaker sections: 12%

### PSLC
- Introduced: 2016
- Tradable PSL achievement certificates

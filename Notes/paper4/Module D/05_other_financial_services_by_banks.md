# Other Financial Services Provided by Banks

============================================================

## 1. Introduction

Apart from traditional banking (deposits & loans),
Banks provide several financial services.

These generate:

Fee-based income
And improve customer retention.

============================================================

# 2. Distribution of Third-Party Products

Banks act as:

Corporate agents
Or distributors.

They distribute products of:

- Insurance companies
- Mutual fund companies
- Pension funds

Bank earns:

Commission income.

============================================================

# 3. Mutual Fund Distribution

Banks distribute:

- Equity funds
- Debt funds
- Hybrid funds
- ELSS funds

Advantages for banks:

- Fee income
- Cross-selling opportunity
- Customer relationship strengthening

============================================================

# 4. Insurance Business

Banks distribute:

- Life insurance
- General insurance
- Health insurance

This model is called:

Bancassurance.

Benefits:

- Additional revenue
- One-stop solution for customers

============================================================

# 5. Social Security Insurance Schemes

Banks promote government-backed schemes such as:

- PMJJBY
- PMSBY
- Atal Pension Yojana

Supports:

Financial inclusion.

============================================================

# 6. Cross Selling

Cross selling means:

Selling additional products
To existing customers.

Example:

Selling insurance to loan customer.

Benefits:

- Higher profitability
- Better customer retention

============================================================

# 7. Depository Services by Banks

Banks provide:

Demat account services
For holding securities electronically.

Functions include:

- Opening Demat accounts
- Settlement of trades
- Custody services

============================================================

# 8. Portfolio Management Services (PMS)

Banks may offer:

Portfolio management for HNIs.

Customised investment solutions
Based on client profile.

============================================================

# 9. Factoring

Factoring is:

Purchase of receivables
By bank or financial institution.

Helps businesses improve liquidity.

============================================================

# 10. Other Agency Business by Banks

Includes:

- Collection of utility bills
- Government payments
- Pension disbursement
- Tax collection
- Sale of gold coins

============================================================

# 11. Importance of Fee-Based Services

- Diversifies income
- Reduces dependence on interest income
- Improves Return on Assets
- Strengthens customer loyalty

============================================================

# 12. Important Exam Focus Areas

- Bancassurance concept
- Third-party product distribution
- Cross-selling meaning
- Depository services
- Factoring concept
- Fee income importance

============================================================

Quick Recall:

Third-party distribution → Commission income  
Bancassurance → Bank + Insurance  
Cross selling → Sell more to same customer  
Demat → Electronic holding of securities

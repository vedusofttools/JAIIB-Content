# Investment Management

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Investment

Investment means:

Committing money today
To generate future income or capital appreciation.

Investment involves:

- Risk
- Return
- Time horizon

============================================================

## 2. Elements of Investment

Every investment has:

- Return (income or appreciation)
- Risk (uncertainty of return)
- Liquidity (ease of conversion to cash)
- Time value of money

Higher return usually involves higher risk.

============================================================

# 3. Basics of Investment Management

Investment management is:

Professional management of assets
To achieve financial goals.

It includes:

- Asset allocation
- Security selection
- Risk management
- Portfolio review

============================================================

# 4. Steps in Investment Management

Step 1:
Define investment objectives.

Step 2:
Assess risk tolerance.

Step 3:
Asset allocation decision.

Step 4:
Selection of securities.

Step 5:
Portfolio construction.

Step 6:
Monitoring and rebalancing.

============================================================

# 5. Investment Banking

Investment banking deals with:

- Corporate finance
- Capital raising
- Mergers & acquisitions
- Underwriting securities

It is different from:

Retail investment management.

============================================================

# 6. Services Offered by Full-Service Investment Banks

- IPO management
- Underwriting
- Advisory services
- Corporate restructuring
- M&A advisory

============================================================

# 7. Investment Management vs Investment Banking

Investment Management:

Focuses on managing client portfolios.

Investment Banking:

Focuses on corporate finance and capital markets.

============================================================

# 8. Portfolio Management

Portfolio management means:

Managing a group of investments
To optimise risk and return.

Objective:

Maximise return for given risk level.

============================================================

## 9. Objectives of Portfolio Management

- Capital appreciation
- Income generation
- Risk diversification
- Capital preservation

============================================================

# 10. Key Elements of Portfolio Management

- Asset allocation
- Diversification
- Risk management
- Performance evaluation
- Rebalancing

============================================================

# 11. Role of Portfolio Manager

Portfolio manager:

- Designs investment strategy
- Monitors performance
- Adjusts portfolio
- Manages risk
- Reports to client

============================================================

# 12. Portfolio Management Services (PMS)

PMS provides:

Customised portfolio management
For high-net-worth clients.

Minimum investment amount prescribed as per regulations.

============================================================

# 13. PMS vs Mutual Funds

PMS:

- Customised
- Higher minimum investment
- Direct ownership of securities

Mutual Fund:

- Pooled investment
- Lower entry amount
- Standardised portfolio

============================================================

# 14. Types of PMS

- Discretionary PMS
- Non-discretionary PMS
- Advisory PMS

============================================================

# 15. Advantages of PMS

- Customised portfolio
- Direct communication
- Flexibility

============================================================

# 16. Disadvantages of PMS

- Higher cost
- Higher risk concentration
- Not suitable for small investors

============================================================

# 17. Recent Developments in PMS in India

- Stricter regulatory norms
- Higher minimum investment limits
- Increased transparency
- Growth in HNI participation

============================================================

# 18. Important Exam Focus Areas

- Investment vs speculation
- Investment management steps
- Portfolio management objectives
- PMS vs Mutual Fund difference
- Role of portfolio manager
- Investment management vs investment banking

============================================================

Investment = Risk + Return

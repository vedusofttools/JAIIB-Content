# Introduction to Wealth Management

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Wealth Management

Wealth Management is:

A comprehensive financial advisory service
That helps individuals
Grow, protect and transfer wealth.

It includes:

- Investment planning
- Tax planning
- Retirement planning
- Estate planning
- Risk management

============================================================

## 2. Importance of Wealth Management

Wealth management is important because:

- Income alone does not ensure financial security.
- Wealth must be preserved and grown.
- Proper planning reduces tax burden.
- Helps achieve long-term goals.
- Ensures smooth wealth transfer.

It supports:

Financial stability and future security.

============================================================

# Digitisation of Retail Banking

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Digitisation in Retail Banking

Digitisation refers to:

Use of technology
To deliver banking services
Through electronic platforms.

It replaces:

Manual processes
With automated systems.

============================================================

## 2. Technology in Retail Banking

Key technologies used:

- Core Banking System (CBS)
- Mobile banking
- Internet banking
- ATM network
- UPI and digital wallets
- Cloud computing
- Data analytics

============================================================

## 3. Technology Processes in Retail Banking

Digitisation enables:

- Online account opening
- Digital KYC
- Automated loan processing
- Real-time transaction monitoring
- E-statements
- Digital signature

============================================================

## 4. Important User-Friendly Features

- 24×7 banking access
- Instant fund transfer
- Mobile alerts
- Self-service portals
- Chatbots
- QR payments

Improves customer experience.

============================================================

## 5. Customer Analytics

Banks use data analytics to:

- Study customer behaviour
- Predict credit risk
- Identify cross-selling opportunities
- Personalise product offerings

Data-driven banking improves profitability.

============================================================

# 6. Institute for Development and Research in Banking Technology (IDRBT)

Established by RBI.

Purpose:

Promote technology in banking sector.

============================================================

# 7. Indian Financial Network (INFINET)

Closed user group network
For banks and financial institutions.

Provides secure communication.

============================================================

# 8. Structured Financial Messaging System (SFMS)

Messaging platform
Developed by IDRBT.

Used for:

Secure financial transactions.

============================================================

# 9. National Financial Switch (NFS)

Operated by NPCI.

Connects:

ATM networks of various banks.

Enables inter-bank ATM transactions.

============================================================

# 10. Indian Banking Community Cloud (IBCC)

Cloud platform
For banking applications.

Enhances:

Scalability
Data security
Cost efficiency

============================================================

# 11. Wealth Management Solutions

Digital platforms for:

- Investment advisory
- Portfolio tracking
- Financial planning

Used in:

Premium retail banking.

============================================================

# 12. Digital Lending

Loan processing through:

- Online applications
- Automated underwriting
- AI-based credit scoring
- Paperless documentation

Faster loan disbursement.

============================================================

# 13. Importance of Digitisation

- Cost reduction
- Operational efficiency
- Better customer engagement
- Fraud detection
- Business expansion

============================================================

# 14. Challenges in Digitisation

- Cybersecurity risk
- Data privacy concerns
- High technology cost
- Digital literacy issues

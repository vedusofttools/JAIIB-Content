# Delivery Channels in Retail Banking

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Delivery Channels

Delivery channels are:

Modes through which banks
Provide services to customers.

They determine:

Customer convenience
Cost efficiency
Service speed

============================================================

## 2. Channel Experience

Channel experience refers to:

Customer’s overall experience
While using banking services.

Includes:

- Ease of access
- Speed of service
- Security
- User-friendliness
- Complaint handling

Positive channel experience increases:

Customer loyalty.

============================================================

# 3. Physical / Direct Channels

============================================================

## A. Branch Banking

Traditional delivery channel.

Features:

- Face-to-face interaction
- Relationship building
- Advisory services
- Complex transaction handling

Advantages:

- Personalised service
- Trust building

Limitations:

- High operational cost
- Limited working hours

============================================================

## B. Automated Teller Machines (ATMs)

Self-service machines for:

- Cash withdrawal
- Balance enquiry
- Mini statement
- Cash deposit (CDM)

Advantages:

- 24×7 availability
- Reduces branch crowd

============================================================

## C. Point of Sale (POS) Terminals

Used for:

Card payments at merchant outlets.

Benefits:

- Promotes digital payments
- Reduces cash usage

============================================================

# 4. Digital Channels

============================================================

## A. Mobile Banking

Banking services through mobile apps.

Features:

- Fund transfer
- Bill payments
- Account management
- Loan application

Highly popular in retail banking.

============================================================

## B. Internet Banking

Banking through web portal.

Services:

- Fund transfer
- Account statement
- Online deposits
- Loan requests

============================================================

# 5. Customer’s Liability on Unauthorized Electronic Transactions

If unauthorized transaction occurs:

Customer must report immediately.

Liability depends on:

- Time of reporting
- Negligence by customer
- Bank system failure

------------------------------------------------------------

General principle:

If reported promptly,
Customer liability may be zero or limited.

If delay in reporting,
Customer may bear limited loss.

Banks must follow:

RBI guidelines on customer protection.

============================================================

# 6. Importance of Multiple Delivery Channels

- Improves accessibility
- Enhances customer satisfaction
- Reduces branch workload
- Promotes financial inclusion
- Lowers transaction cost

============================================================

# 7. Challenges in Delivery Channels

- Cybersecurity threats
- System downtime
- Fraud risk
- Customer digital literacy gap

============================================================

# Marketing Information Systems (MKIS)

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of MKIS

Marketing Information System (MKIS) is:

A structured system
For collecting, analysing and distributing
Marketing-related information
To support decision-making.

It helps management take:

Data-driven marketing decisions.

============================================================

## 2. Functions of MKIS

- Collect market data
- Analyse customer trends
- Monitor competitors
- Support pricing decisions
- Assist promotional planning
- Evaluate marketing performance

============================================================

## 3. Components of MKIS

MKIS consists of:

1. Internal Records System  
   (Sales data, deposit growth, loan data)

2. Marketing Intelligence System  
   (Competitor information, market trends)

3. Marketing Research System  
   (Customer surveys, feedback)

4. Decision Support System  
   (Analytical tools and models)

============================================================

## 4. The MKIS Model

Data Collection → Data Processing → Analysis → Reporting → Decision Making → Feedback

Continuous feedback improves marketing strategy.

============================================================

## 5. Usage of Computers in MKIS

Computers help in:

- Data storage
- Data analysis
- Forecasting
- Generating reports
- Predictive modelling

Technology improves speed and accuracy.

============================================================

## 6. Support for Marketing Management

MKIS helps managers in:

- Planning
- Organising
- Controlling
- Performance evaluation

Improves strategic marketing decisions.

============================================================

## 7. Support for Marketing Mix Decisions

MKIS supports decisions on:

- Product modification
- Pricing strategy
- Promotion campaigns
- Distribution channels

============================================================

## 8. Use of Decision Models

Banks use:

Statistical models
Forecasting tools
Trend analysis
Customer segmentation models

To improve marketing outcomes.

============================================================

## 9. Performance of MKIS

Effective MKIS should be:

- Accurate
- Timely
- Relevant
- Cost-effective
- User-friendly

============================================================

## 10. Recommendations for Effective MKIS

- Integrate with core banking system
- Maintain updated database
- Ensure data security
- Provide staff training
- Use analytics tools

============================================================

## 11. Advantages of MKIS

- Better market understanding
- Improved customer targeting
- Increased profitability
- Enhanced competitive advantage
- Improved marketing efficiency

============================================================

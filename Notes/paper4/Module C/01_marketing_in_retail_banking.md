# Marketing in Retail Banking

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Marketing

Marketing is:

The process of identifying,
Anticipating and satisfying
Customer needs profitably.

It is not just selling.

It includes:

- Product planning
- Pricing
- Promotion
- Distribution
- Customer relationship

============================================================

## 2. Marketing in Retail Banking

In retail banking, marketing means:

Designing and promoting banking products
To meet customer requirements
And generate profitability.

Retail banking marketing focuses on:

- Individuals
- Small businesses
- Mass customer base

============================================================

## 3. Objectives of Marketing in Banking

- Attract new customers
- Retain existing customers
- Increase cross-selling
- Enhance customer satisfaction
- Improve profitability
- Strengthen brand image

============================================================

# 4. Marketing Mix in Retail Banking (7 Ps)

In services sector like banking,
Marketing mix consists of 7 elements.

------------------------------------------------------------

1. Product

Deposit products, loans, cards,
Digital services, insurance, etc.

------------------------------------------------------------

2. Price

Interest rates,
Service charges,
Processing fees.

Pricing must balance:

Profitability + Competition.

------------------------------------------------------------

3. Place

Delivery channels:

Branches, ATMs, mobile banking,
Internet banking, agents.

------------------------------------------------------------

4. Promotion

Advertising,
Personal selling,
Digital marketing,
Sales campaigns.

------------------------------------------------------------

5. People

Bank staff,
Relationship managers,
Customer service personnel.

Service quality depends heavily on people.

------------------------------------------------------------

6. Process

Account opening process,
Loan processing,
Complaint handling.

Smooth processes improve satisfaction.

------------------------------------------------------------

7. Physical Evidence

Branch layout,
ATM facilities,
Website design,
Brand image.

============================================================

## 5. Characteristics of Banking Marketing

- Intangible service
- Trust-based relationship
- Long-term engagement
- High competition
- Regulation-driven

============================================================

## 6. Importance of Marketing in Retail Banking

- Improves market share
- Enhances customer loyalty
- Increases deposit mobilisation
- Boosts loan growth
- Supports cross-selling

## 7. Modern Trends in Banking Marketing

- Personalised offers
- AI-based recommendations

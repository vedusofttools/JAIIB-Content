# Retail Banking vs Corporate / Wholesale Banking

============================================================

## 1. Applicability of Retail Banking Concepts

Retail banking concepts focus on:

- Large number of individual customers
- Small ticket size transactions
- Standardised products
- High volume operations
- Technology-driven delivery

These concepts are applicable in:

- Savings and deposit mobilisation
- Consumer loans
- Small business financing
- Cross-selling financial products

Retail banking emphasises:

Customer relationship
Mass marketing
Service efficiency

============================================================

# 2. Corporate / Wholesale Banking

Corporate Banking refers to:

Banking services provided
To large corporations
Government entities
And institutions.

Wholesale banking deals with:

Large-value transactions
And institutional clients.

============================================================

# 3. Key Differences

------------------------------------------------------------

Customer Base:

Retail → Individuals & small businesses  
Corporate → Large companies & institutions

------------------------------------------------------------

Loan Size:

Retail → Small ticket size  
Corporate → Large ticket size

------------------------------------------------------------

Risk Profile:

Retail → Diversified risk  
Corporate → Concentrated risk

------------------------------------------------------------

Revenue Pattern:

Retail → High volume, low margin  
Corporate → Low volume, high value

------------------------------------------------------------

Documentation:

Retail → Standardised  
Corporate → Customised

------------------------------------------------------------

Processing:

Retail → Automated & technology-driven  
Corporate → Relationship-based & specialised

============================================================

# 4. Risk Comparison

Retail Banking:

Risk spread across many customers.
Default by one customer has limited impact.

Corporate Banking:

Large exposure to single borrower.
Higher credit concentration risk.

============================================================

# 5. Income Pattern Comparison

Retail:

Stable interest income.
Significant fee income from cross-selling.

Corporate:

Interest income from large loans.
Fee income from project finance, trade finance, treasury.

============================================================

# 6. Operational Complexity

Retail:

High transaction volume.
Requires strong IT systems.

Corporate:

Complex credit appraisal.
Requires financial expertise.

============================================================

# 7. Role in Bank Strategy

Retail banking provides:

Stable deposit base
Recurring income

Corporate banking provides:

High-value lending opportunities
Strategic client relationships

Balanced portfolio improves bank stability.

============================================================

# 8. Importance in Indian Banking Context

After economic reforms:

Banks shifted focus toward retail segment
To reduce concentration risk.

Retail lending now forms significant portion
Of bank loan portfolio.

============================================================

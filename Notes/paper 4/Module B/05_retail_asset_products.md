# Retail Asset Products

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Retail Asset Products

Retail Asset Products are:

Loans and advances
Granted to individuals
For personal or consumption purposes.

These generate:

Interest income for the bank.

============================================================

# 2. Home Loans

Home loan is granted for:

- Purchase of house
- Construction of house
- Purchase of flat
- Plot + construction

------------------------------------------------------------

Features:

- Long tenure (up to 20–30 years)
- Secured by mortgage
- Floating or fixed interest rate
- EMI repayment

============================================================

# 3. Housing Loan under PMAY

Under:

Prime Minister’s Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Eligible borrowers receive:

Interest subsidy under Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS).

Purpose:

Promote affordable housing.

============================================================

# 4. Home Improvement / Extension Loans

Loan granted for:

- Renovation
- Repair
- Interior modification
- Extension of house

Usually smaller amount
Compared to home purchase loan.

============================================================

# 5. Home Decor Loans

Short-term loan for:

Furniture
Interior decoration
Appliances

Often unsecured or partially secured.

============================================================

# 6. Auto / Vehicle Loans

Loan for purchase of:

- Two-wheelers
- Cars
- Commercial vehicles

Vehicle hypothecated to bank.

Short to medium tenure.

============================================================

# 7. Personal Loans

Unsecured loans
For personal expenses.

Features:

- No collateral
- Higher interest rate
- Short tenure
- Quick processing

Riskier for banks.

============================================================

# 8. Educational Loans

Loan for:

- Higher education in India or abroad

Repayment starts:

After course completion + moratorium period.

Often supported by:

Government schemes.

============================================================

# 9. Other Retail Loans

- Gold loans
- Consumer durable loans
- Loan against property
- Loan against securities

============================================================

# 10. Processing of Retail Loans

Retail loan processing involves:

1. Application submission
2. KYC verification
3. Credit score check
4. Income assessment
5. Field verification
6. Legal & technical verification (for home loan)
7. Sanction
8. Documentation
9. Disbursement

============================================================

# 11. Risk Assessment in Retail Lending

Banks assess:

- Repayment capacity
- Credit history
- Employment stability
- Debt-to-income ratio
- Collateral value

============================================================

# 12. Importance of Retail Loans for Banks

- Diversified credit risk
- Higher yield compared to corporate loans
- Stable income stream
- Cross-selling opportunities

============================================================

# 13. Important Exam Focus Areas

- Secured vs unsecured loans
- PMAY concept
- Hypothecation meaning
- Loan processing steps
- Moratorium period in education loans
- Gold loan security

============================================================

Quick Recall:

Home loan → Mortgage  
Vehicle loan → Hypothecation

# Retail Liability Products

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Retail Liability Products

Liability products are:

Deposit products
Through which banks mobilise funds.

Deposits form major source of funds for banks.

============================================================

# A. Demand Deposits

Demand deposits are:

Withdrawable on demand
Without prior notice.

Types:

- Current Account
- Savings Bank Account

============================================================

# 2. Current Deposit Account

## Meaning

Current account is:

Non-interest bearing account
Mainly for business entities.

============================================================

## Features of Current Account

- No interest paid
- Unlimited transactions
- Overdraft facility available
- Minimum balance requirement
- Used for business transactions

============================================================

## Advantages of Current Account

- Easy business payments
- High liquidity
- Cheque facility
- Online banking facility

============================================================

## Documents Required

For individuals:

- KYC documents

For firms/companies:

- PAN
- Partnership deed
- MOA/AOA
- Board resolution
- GST registration (if applicable)

============================================================

## Operational Instructions

- Joint operation (Either or Survivor)
- Mandate holder
- Stop payment instructions
- Account transfer between branches

============================================================

## Inoperative Accounts

If no transaction for prescribed period,
Account becomes inoperative.

Banks monitor such accounts carefully.

============================================================

## Closure of Current Account

- Written request
- Settlement of dues
- Return of unused cheque leaves

============================================================

# 3. Savings Bank Account

## Meaning

Savings account is:

Interest-bearing deposit account
For individuals.

============================================================

## Who Can Open?

- Individuals
- Minors
- Joint holders
- HUF
- Some organisations

============================================================

## Advantages

- Earn interest
- Safe deposit
- ATM & digital access
- Easy fund transfer

============================================================

## Deregulation of Interest

RBI deregulated savings deposit interest rate.

Banks now decide interest rate.

============================================================

## Restrictions

- Limited withdrawals (in some cases)
- Minimum balance requirement
- Non-business usage

============================================================

# 4. Time Deposits (Term Deposits)

Time deposits are:

Deposits for fixed period.

Types:

- Fixed Deposit (FD)
- Recurring Deposit (RD)

============================================================

# 5. Fixed Deposit (FD)

## Features

- Fixed tenure
- Higher interest rate
- Premature withdrawal allowed (with penalty)

============================================================

## Interest on FD

- Calculated at contracted rate
- Compounded quarterly (generally)

============================================================

## Premature Payment

Allowed with:

Penalty on interest rate.

============================================================

## Renewal of FD

On maturity:

- Renewed
- Paid out
- Converted to savings

============================================================

## RBI Rule on Overdue FD

If not renewed,
Interest paid at:

Savings rate or contracted rate
As per RBI guidelines.

============================================================

# 6. Recurring Deposit (RD)

- Monthly instalment deposit
- Fixed tenure
- Suitable for salaried individuals

============================================================

# 7. Advances Against Own Deposits

Banks may grant:

Loan against FD/RD.

Interest charged slightly higher
Than deposit rate.

============================================================

# 8. Form 15G / 15H

Submitted to avoid TDS on interest.

15G → Individuals below 60  
15H → Senior citizens

============================================================

# 9. TDS on Deposits

If interest exceeds prescribed limit,
Bank deducts TDS.

Bank must deposit TDS
With Income Tax Department.

============================================================

# 10. 2-in-1 Account

Savings account linked with FD.

Surplus automatically transferred to FD.

Helps earn higher interest.

============================================================

# 11. Important Exam Focus Areas

- Current vs Savings account difference
- Deregulation of SB interest
- FD premature rules
- Form 15G/15H
- Loan against FD
- Inoperative account meaning
- 2-in-1 account concept

============================================================

Quick Recall:

Current → No interest  
Savings → Interest bearing  
FD → Fixed tenure  
RD → Monthly deposit  
15G/15H → Avoid TDS  
Loan against FD possible

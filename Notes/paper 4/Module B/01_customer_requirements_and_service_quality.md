# Customer Requirements and Service Quality

============================================================

## 1. Meaning of Customer Requirements in Retail Banking

Customer requirements refer to:

Needs, expectations and preferences
Of customers from banking services.

Retail banking must focus on:

- Convenience
- Speed
- Safety
- Accessibility
- Transparency
- Cost-effectiveness

Customer satisfaction determines:

Bank’s success and retention level.

============================================================

# 2. Maslow’s Theory and Customer Requirements

Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs explains:

Human needs are arranged in levels.

From lowest to highest:

1. Physiological Needs
2. Safety Needs
3. Social Needs
4. Esteem Needs
5. Self-Actualisation Needs

------------------------------------------------------------

### Application in Retail Banking

Physiological Needs:
Basic banking access (savings account).

Safety Needs:
Safe deposits, fraud protection.

Social Needs:
Relationship banking, personalised service.

Esteem Needs:
Premium banking, priority services.

Self-Actualisation:
Wealth management, investment advisory.

Banks design products based on these layers.

============================================================

# 4. Service Quality in Retail Banking

Service quality determines:

Customer satisfaction
Customer loyalty
Brand reputation

Poor service leads to:

Customer attrition.

============================================================

# 5. Dimensions of Service Quality (SERVQUAL Model)

Service quality measured through:

1. Tangibility  
   (Physical facilities, staff appearance)

2. Reliability  
   (Accurate and dependable service)

3. Responsiveness  
   (Prompt service)

4. Assurance  
   (Knowledge and trustworthiness of staff)

5. Empathy  
   (Individual attention to customers)

============================================================

# 6. Importance of Service Quality in Retail Banking

- Improves customer retention
- Enhances cross-selling
- Increases profitability
- Builds trust
- Reduces complaints
